Non-SSOM Issue Management Responsibilities Matrix

Key DRI Definitions

For non-SSOM issues, the following are the key DRIs defined by the Issue Management Program:

1 Identifying Party

First to detect the issue; responsible for logging in Protecht or notifying the accountable function to do so.

2 Issue Owner

Reflects area where root cause occurred; accountable for end-to-end issue remediation.

3 Action Plan Owner

Responsible for executing specific remediation steps.

4 Issue/Action Plan Approver

Provides independent oversight of issue/action plan execution; confirms remediation is complete and supporting evidence is sufficient for closure.
